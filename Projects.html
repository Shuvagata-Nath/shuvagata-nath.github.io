Braille-X: Refreshable Mechanical Braille Display

Electromechanical System Design Coursework

  • Designed and 3D-printed custom octagonal Braille disks tailored for a low-cost refreshable display, ensuring precise tactile feedback for visually impaired users.
  • Developed robust text-to-Braille algorithms to accurately convert digital text into tactile Braille characters, optimizing readability and minimizing translation errors.
  • Engineered a linear motion system using stepper motors to align disks precisely, enabling smooth sequential Braille output and improving user interaction speed and reliability.
